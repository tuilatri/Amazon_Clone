SECTION 3: SCALABILITY & FUTURE GROWTH
================================================================================

3.1 SCENARIO: USER COUNT INCREASES (10K → 100K Users)
------------------------------------------------------
○ MODERATE IMPACT
  Current: "Total Customers: 2,002" displays fine
  At scale: Still fine as a simple count
  
  Recommendation: Eventually add "Active users (30 days)" metric
  Priority: LOW

3.2 SCENARIO: ORDER VOLUME GROWS (100 → 10,000 Orders)
-------------------------------------------------------
⚠ POTENTIAL CONCERN
  Current: Status counts query on every refresh
  At scale: GROUP BY on large table could slow down
  
  Mitigation options:
  - Add database index on order_status_id
  - Consider cached/pre-aggregated counts
  - Implement materialized views
  
  Priority: MEDIUM (address when reaching 5K+ orders)

3.3 SCENARIO: MULTIPLE SUPPLIERS EXIST
---------------------------------------
○ LOW IMPACT
  Current: Overview is admin-only, doesn't show supplier breakdown
  At scale: May need "Orders by Supplier" section
  
  Recommendation: Future User/Supplier Management tabs will handle this
  Priority: LOW (out of scope for Overview)
